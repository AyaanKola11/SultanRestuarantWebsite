About Us

At Sultan, we invite you to embark on a culinary adventure through the vibrant flavors and rich cultural tapestry of the Middle East. Nestled in the heart of Midrand, our restaurant is more than just a dining destination; it's a celebration of tradition, heritage, and hospitality.
